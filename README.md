## 👩‍💻 About

I completed an internship at STI–UFRN, the information technology (IT) department of the Federal University of Rio Grande do Norte (UFRN).

During this period, I conducted **quality control tests** for internal computer systems developed by the department, focusing on:

🔹 Functional verification

🔹 Usability evaluation

🔹 Regression testing

My work contributed to enhancing the usability and reliability of several institutional platforms used across UFRN and other Federal Institutions.


## 🧪 Tools & Methods

- **Trello**: Task tracking and test case management

- **Jira**: Defect tracking and team collaboration

- **Manual Testing**: Test case design and execution

- **Selenium**: Introduction to automation concepts

- **Documentation**: Test reports and results summary


## 🚀 What I Delivered

**Improved system reliability** through comprehensive testing

**Reduced defects** via systematic regression testing

**Enhanced team communication** with clear bug reports

**Strong documentation** for future maintenance

Collaborated with **QA insights** in SDLC team environments
